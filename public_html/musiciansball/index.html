Welcome

The Gamma Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi will once again be hosting a Musician's Ball! Come enjoy an evening of food, music, and dancing with us! All musicians associated with the University of Washington are invited to join us on May 10th, 2019.

The theme is 1920's Jazz, so dress accordingly!

Tickets are $10 in advance or $14 at the door. Doors open at 7:30!

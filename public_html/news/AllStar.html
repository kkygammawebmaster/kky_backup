Seattle All-Star Band

The Seattle All Star Band is a great opportunity for beginning band students in fourth to eighth grade. Come play in a concert band with college musicians for the day! The event takes place on Sunday, May 29th at 1:00 pm in the Meany Hall, Room 268 at the University of Washington.

Register Here


Students

If you’ve ever wanted to play side-by-side with college musicians, now’s your chance! We’ll rehearse in a reading band together, where we sight read several pieces of music and rehearse for a short period of time. Then you’ll get to perform what you’ve just rehearsed for your parents. We’ll record the performance and burn a CD you get to take home with you. For your reference, this is last year’s recording.

 

Parents

While your kids are rehearsing with us, you’ll have the opportunity to participate in a parent workshop. The first part will focus on giving you the tools to help your kids continue with music, whether that be in school, in the community, or just with their friends. The second part of the workshop is a question and answer session with college students where we’re happy to talk about anything music-related. Whether you’re a seasoned parent of many musicians or are unsure how to support your son or daughter, you’re bound to learn something new.

Schedule

May 29th in Meany Hall, Room 268 at UW

  • 1:00 pm – Registration starts
  • 1:30 pm – Rehearsal begins
  • 3:30 pm – Performance for parents
  • 4:00 PM – Event ends

About Us — Contact

We are the Gamma chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi, a national co-ed honorary band service fraternity. We exist to promote and serve bands at the University of Washington and in the community. Our members come from a variety of majors and musical backgrounds.

If you have any questions about this event, please email Sarah Doty at kkygammaservice@gmail.com.

Directions

This event will be hosted in Meany Hall, Room 268 in the University of Washington. Follow I5 until you get to the University of Washington exit, on NE 45th St., after around half a mile you will be on the edge of the University of Washington campus. Since this was moved to Meany Hall, we recommend parking in the Central Campus Parking Garage, beneath Red Square on the Campus Map.

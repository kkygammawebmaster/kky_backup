Senior Spotlight – Class of 2018

Published by Austin on June 18, 2018

Now that the seniors have graduated, Gamma would like to recognize and congratulate our newest Alumni. Thank you for all you’ve done in your years as Actives!

Brian Huynh

Alpha Zeta
Schunke

Bachelor of Science (Anthropology)


Brian plans on volunteering at the Seattle Municipal Court and working after graduation!

“Thank you Gamma for providing me a wonderful place to find myself and meet exceptionally wonderful people.”






Dustine Bowker

Alpha Theta
Rogers

Bachelor of the Arts (Law, Societies, and Justice)

Bachelor of Arts (Political Science)


Dustine plans on taking a gap year to find an entry-level position at a law firm and preparing to take the LSAT so he can become a Disability Rights Attorney!

“It has been an honor to be surrounded by an amazing, supportive group like the Gamma Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi. I have met some of my closest friends through this organization, and it has been a great experience being a part of it. To those who have supported me at any point during my time in HMB and Gamma, you are greatly appreciated for what you have done for me. Go Dawgs!”

Alpha Eta

Informatics minor

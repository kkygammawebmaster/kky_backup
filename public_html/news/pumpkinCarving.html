Pumpkin Carving

Published by admin on October 30, 2014

We sure love Autumn here in Seattle! Had such a great time enjoying the view, carving pumpkins and hosting a successful recruitment event this past Sunday, 10/26.

Fun fact: Pumpkin carving has been a Gamma tradition for more than 10 years. The earliest mention via email is from 2003, but we’re sure that this event has been going on since before the chapter even started using email!

Junior/Senior Day

Welcome to Junior/Senior Day, hosted by the Husky Marching Band! Experience what college band life will be like by spending the day with the Husky Band and Cheer as we perform for the UW Football Spring Game. We invite you to join us in our performance at Husky Stadium (the greatest setting in college football), hang out with current members, and learn about all the amazing opportunities that college band has to offer!

Date: 8:30 am April 23, 2016

Location: Husky Stadium

Registration Deadline: April 15th, 2016

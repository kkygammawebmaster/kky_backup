Seattle All-Star Band.

Welcome to the first-ever Seattle All-Star Band, where young musicians get to play with college students. Taking place on February 15th in the Music Building at the University of Washington, this is a great way to play with people from the Seattle area. Register below and scroll down for more details.

Students

If you've ever wanted to play side-by-side with college musicians, now's your chance! We'll rehearse in a reading band together, where we sight read several pieces of music and rehearse for a short period of time. Then you'll get to perform what you've just rehearsed for your parents. We'll record the performance and while we're burning a CD you get to take home with you, several small ensembles will play a variety of music.

If you will need to check out an instrument to play, please use the link below to sign up.

Instrument Sign Up

Parents

While your kids are rehearsing with us, you'll have the opportunity to participate in a parent workshop. The first part will focus on giving you the tools to help your kids continue with music, whether that be in school, in the community, or just with their friends. The second part of the workshop is a question and answer session with college students where we're happy to talk about anything music-related. Whether you're a seasoned parent of many musicians or are unsure how to support your son or daughter, you're bound to learn something new.

About Us -- Contact

We are the Gamma chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi, a national co-ed honorary band service fraternity. We exist to promote and serve bands at the University of Washington and in the community. Our members come from a variety of majors and musical backgrounds. For this event, we will be joined by other Kappa Kappa Psi and Tau Beta Sigma (our sister organization) chapters from Oregon, California, Utah, Idaho, and Arizona.

If you have any questions about this event, please email Grey Larson at kkygammaservice@gmail.com

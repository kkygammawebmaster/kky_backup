Kappa Kappa Psi Gamma


Gamma

Based at the University of Washington in Seattle, the Gamma Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi has been hard at work serving bands throughout its existence. Originally chartered on December 16, 1920 the Gamma chapter was re-chartered on July 10, 1993.You can learn more about us under the Gamma Chapter History tab.
We pride ourselves on a strong sense of tradition and class which allows us to always strive for the highest.
